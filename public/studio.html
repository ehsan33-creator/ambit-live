FAQ

Questions, answered

 

Do I need my own AI API key?

Yes — Ambit connects to your own OpenAI, Anthropic, Google AI or OpenRouter account. You pay providers directly at cost, and Ambit shows an estimate before every generation. The platform never uses shared credentials.

What sources can I generate from?

Plain prompts, PDF, DOCX, PPTX, TXT, Markdown, website URLs, images (including whiteboard photos and diagrams), audio and video recordings, and batches of multiple files at once.

Can I edit what the AI produces?

Everything is editable — question text, options, correct answers, explanations, difficulty, tags and order. The AI assistant can rewrite, translate, or extend questions, and every change is undoable.

Which languages are supported?

English, Malay, Chinese, Arabic, French, Spanish, Japanese, German, Tamil, Hindi and more — including automatic translation and bilingual assessments.

How do exports work with my LMS?

Export to QTI, Moodle XML, SCORM packages, or Google Forms, alongside PDF, DOCX, CSV, Excel and JSON — with optional answer keys, marking schemes and rubric sheets.
